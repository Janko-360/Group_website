Orbital splash

Height Requirement: Between 132cm and 198 cm

Thrill Rating: Moderate

Ride Description

Go inside the eye of insanity in Orbital Splash, a mind-blowingly original water adventure like nothing you’ve ever seen. You’re going to get good and wet on this one, because Orbital Splash goes through 5,000 gallons of water a minute.

Once you grab one of the specially designed four-person rafts and climb seven stories up to the top of the tower, you’re ready to jump back to earth. You’ll also want to grab a few friends or family for the ride, that is if you’ve got friends or family along who are brave enough to give it a go.

First you’ll shoot from a height of 75 feet down a 132-foot tunnel. Right about now you’re traveling at 35 feet per second. But hold on tight, because you are about to be shot into an unbelievably huge 60-foot-wide funnel, where you’ll feel the full thundering power of gravity as you orbit back to earth.

Your raft swishes up the impossibly high walls at full blast and you’ll be powerless to do anything about it. So hang on while you slide wildly back and forth, until you’re all shot out the bottom. Sharing this mind-bending, pulse-racing, world class thrill with your friends is what Cyber Splash is all about.

Height Requirement: Between 132cm and 198 cm

Thrill Rating: Mild

Ride Description

Getting tossed around in big waves is always fun. Now you can surf the unpredictable waves of crypto currency in our world class wave pool. Crypto Surf is a massive wave pool that generates ocean-size waves, in your own VIP ocean right in the middle of Cyber Splash. Surf’s up!

At a million gallons, there’s plenty of room for you to spread your flippers and get your wave on. Take a raft and ride over each four-foot crest like a ship at sea, or brave it freestyle, bobbing on the surf like a pro. Either way, this wave pool is a serious fun.

You can relax in the shallow end if ripples are more your style, so your little fish can find just the right-sized wave. But when you swim deeper in the pool, as the water starts to rise, you’ll rise with it. One after another you’ll be swamped with wave after wave, pummeled and jiggled while you laugh. Surrounded by good vibes, spend a day in the ocean without ever leaving Cybersplash. Floating up and down in these super fun waves is good for you too. You’ll stay cool as a fish on a hot summer day.

Binary Tunnels

Height Requirement: Between 132cm and 198 cm

Thrill Rating: Moderate

Ride Description

Get ready to race head-first down 1,800 feet of slide on an aero-dynamically designed water toboggan. You can challenge your friends and family to an extreme race on our 2 slide complex, Binary Tunnels. Choosing which one of the two steep, slick, and snaking slides to ride down may be more challenging, because they are both incredibly fun.

Whichever slide you pick, this 4-story tall complex features 2 lanes that will send you and your competitors barreling head-first down several steep drops before crossing the finish line. You’re in for a crazy time as you plummet down a ridiculously steep 64-foot slope together into the pool.

Don’t worry, real friends won’t bust you for screaming.

Height Requirement: Between 132cm and 198 cm

Thrill Rating: Extreme

Ride Description

Who needs a raft? Your own body will do just fine on this pulse-quickening, blazingly fast slide.

Keep walking up higher, higher, higher. Trust us, you’ll eventually get to the top — it’s nearly a whopping eight stories in the air! Now it’s decision time, are you really brave enough?

The Wetrunner is 357 feet of pure adrenaline, in the form of a super slick enclosed slide. Survive snaking turns in total darkness with only your own body to maneuver the curves. Just brace yourself for the 64-foot elevation change in a few seconds. Though really, how prepared can one ever be for their body dropping 65 feet through a dark watery tunnel at 40 miles per hour? Hopefully that warm summer air will muffle your screams.

Lazy Eddies

Height Requirement: Between 132cm and 198 cm

Thrill Rating: Moderate

Ride Description

What’s the rush? You deserve some rest and relaxation, and what better place to find it than on a Lazy Eddies cruise. This slow-moving water trail, at a depth of only 3 ½ feet, weaves around the park and is just what you need when it is time to slow down and enjoy life at a mellow pace. Set sail on a tour of Cyber Splash, destination: relaxation.

Hop on a raft and let the gentle current do all the work. You’ll see all the sights, like Orbital Splash and The Wetrunner towering off in either direction, as you cruise at three miles per hour along the slow-flow ride. The river also passes Crypto Surf and Binary Tunnels.

Sure there’s plenty of time to get off the raft and splash around with your friends. Or you could just do what Lazy Eddies was designed for— kick back, put on some shades, and take a long, relaxing rest on the water, with nothing to get between you and total tranquility.
