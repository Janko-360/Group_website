Safety protocols

CyberSplash has made every effort to comply with new safety regulations to combat the Corona Virus crisis. We adhere to all safety regulations passed by the relevant health authorities. In doing so, we have established new and effective protocols to keep everyone safe whilst having a good time.

Accessibility

Everyone should have the opportunity to experience the thrill of our world-class rides. To ensure this, we have modified rafts and ride seats to accommodate those who are wheelchair-bound. Accessibility was our number-one priority when designing our park layout. All rides and amenities are accessible via ramps or elevators. Feel free to approach our friendly members of staff for assistance.

Guest Safety

We are a Park that values your safety. Our mission is for you to enjoy every ride, every slide and all we have to offer. To ensure this our members of staff have undergone rigorous training. The pool water undergoes a meticulous cleaning procedure using cutting edge sanitization technologies. All rides are built with PCSA (Park Construction Security Authority) materials and passed all tests with flying colours.

So you have nothing to worry about.

Park Policy

To ensure that all guests have the time of their lives, we have enacted a few rules and regulations. They serve all guests and staff.

We ask all visitors and staff to adhere to the following:

  • No dangerous or irrational behaviour when on a ride or visiting the premises. Accidents happen faster than anyone can anticipate.
  • Please refrain from the use of any rides if feeling sick or unwell. Protect yourselves and each other from infection.

Accommodation

After a long fun-filled day you can relax and refresh at one of our many restaurants. We have an exceptional collection. From our fast and easy snack bar to our premium 4-star restaurant, whatever your heart, or stomach, desires we will strive to achieve. After feasting, we invite you to retire to one of our spacious en-suite hotel rooms. All accommodation is maintained to the highest of standards; we will do everything we can to help you enjoy your stay.
